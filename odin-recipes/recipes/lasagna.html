Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe features layers of savory meat sauce, tender noodles, and a rich,
cheesy ricotta filling. Assemble by layering sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture in a 9x13 dish,
finishing with mozzarella, then bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45-60 minutes, covering with foil
initially to ensure perfectly melted results.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24-45 oz) marinara or pasta sauce
  • 1 tsp dried basil/oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1 large egg (beaten)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (cooked al dente)
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Extra Parmesan for top

Steps

  1. Prepare Meat Sauce: Brown the ground beef and onions in a skillet; drain fat. Add garlic, marinara sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and parmesan cheese.
  3. Preheat & Prep: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.

  4. Layer the Lasagna:

    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of the dish.
    2. Add a layer of noodles.
    3. Spread a layer of ricotta mixture, followed by mozzarella cheese and meat sauce.
    4. Repeat layers (noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, meat) until ingredients are used, ending with sauce and a generous layer of mozzarella.
  5. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil (tip: spray the foil with cooking spray to prevent sticking). Bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Rest & Serve: Let the lasagna rest for at least 15-30 minutes before cutting to help it hold its shape.
